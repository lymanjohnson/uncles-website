Birth abt 1177
Death aft 1236 Age: 59
Misc. Notes
He was an adult by 1198 when, as William de Ralegh, he granted to William Briwar the land which his uncle Walter de Ralegh had held. In 1225 and 1233 he was sheriff of Devon. In 1235-6 William de Ral' held one fee in Devon which may have been that held earlier by Hught de Ralegh in Alvrington.
