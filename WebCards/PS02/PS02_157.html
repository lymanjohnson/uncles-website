Will
To the poor of Hedcorne. To a minister to preach at my funeral. To Elizabeth Joseph, my daughter. To Jane Fowle, my youngest daughter, £300. To my daughter-in-law [ie. step-daughter] Mary Stannard [which may be her married name and not her birth name]. To the widow Perrin. To my grandchild Richard Borden my little house and land in the east end of Hedcorne which I bought of Abraham Coaras[?], and his mother, Joane Borthen, is to take the profits so long as she lives. To Joane Borthen, my daughter, an annuity of 40s. a year out of my lands in Frittenden which I bought of William Matthews. The rest of my lands to my son Richard Fowle. Proved 26 Mar 1631.
