Misc. Notes
She probably married Manoah Ellis about 1679. Manoah was born about 1659. Manoah was the son of Lieut. John Ellis and Elizabeth Freeman. He died 30 August 1748. Manoah was a defendant in a court case at the March Court 1679/80 in Plymouth Colony. "Manoah Ellis and Mary his wife, of Sandwich were accused of premarital relations for which they were convicted and fined £5." (See Manoah Ellis [http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.com/~burgessgen/i0091480.htm#i91480] for the continuation of this line.)195
