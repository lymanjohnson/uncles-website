Misc. Notes
Born ca 1597 in England. John was baptized in Droitwich, Worcestershire on 18 Apr 1597.1 John died in bet 12 Mar 1673/4 and 21 May 1674.1 Occupation: merchant.

John came to Plymouth in the Fortune in 1621. He resided for a time at Marshfield on the estate at Careswell, but removed to Boston in 1657, where a little later he purchased a house on Spring Lane.

John was representative 1653 and two years more. In 1657 he removed to Boston, and was a thrifty mercahnt. He was freeman 1762.

On 22 May 1627 John married Mary Chilton (266) , daughter of James Chilton (96) (bef 1553-8 Dec 1620), in Plymouth, MA. Born ca 1607 in England. Mary was baptized in St Peter's Parish, Sandwich on 31 May 1607.12 Mary died bet 31 Jul 1676 and 1 May 1679.

Mary is traditionally said to be the first European to set foot on Plymouth Rock.
