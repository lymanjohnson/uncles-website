,566He was in Norwalk, Connecticut, by 1671 and received land from the town in 1677 for service as a soldier in King Philip's War. On October 21, 1674, he received his portion of his father's estate. He married Mary St. John in Norwalk on November 10, 1677, daughter of Matthias St. John. Their name was usually spelled Sention before 1700.
