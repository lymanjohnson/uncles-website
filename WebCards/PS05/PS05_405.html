Misc. Notes
Buried 11 Feb. 1635 at Cranfield, Bedfordshire. His will is dated 7 Dec. 1627 with a codicil 18 June 1633, proved 24 Feb 1635. It is published in full in "The American Genealogist," Vol. 14, pp. 2-4. It refers to his house at Borne End. He was called "of the blakehouse" in the burial entry. The will is on file in Birmingham, England.

William Baker of Cranfield, in his will of 25 April 1621, appointed his cousin Thomas Wheeler of Borne End as overseer. 550, p52
