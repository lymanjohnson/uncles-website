Will
To the poor of Frittendon 20s. To Thomas Fowle, my son and executor, all my moveables, but £10 is to be distributed between my son Thomas and my daughters Alice and Joane. To John Donner, my daughter's son, 20s. To my son Thomas Fowle my lands in Fryttenden and Marden, he to pay my two daughters Alice and Joane 13s. 4d. yearly for their lives and to his daughter Joane Fowle 20s. yearly after she reaches the age of 22 years, and for default she may enter. Witnesses: John Pett the Elder, John Pett the Younger, his son and, and William Foldes. Proved 20 Oct 1572. (Archdeaconry of Canterbury, vol. 41, fo. 277.)648, v75p228
