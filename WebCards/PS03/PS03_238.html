Will
To be buried in the churchyard of St. Mighioll at Marden. To the high altar for tythes forgotten. To the said light 12d. Residue to my wife Johane, executrix. To my wife Johane my lands for life, and then to my daughter Costaunce. If my wife is with a son, then my lands are to remain to the said son. If my children decease before my wife, then to my brother's children. Proved 7 Nov 1514. (Archdeaconry of Canterbury, vol. 12, fo. 370)648, v75p227
