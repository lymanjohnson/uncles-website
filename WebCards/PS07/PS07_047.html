Misc. Notes
Thomas French, b. abt 1635, married Mary Adams, and they had four daughters and three sons in Ipswich. Their only son with children wasWilliam, who moved to New Hampshire. William married Abigail Wiggin andtheir six sons, including Bradstreet French, all had children. The descendants of William and Abigail are numerous, and tended to remain in New Hampshire and neighboring states. [FFA Chart 1]
