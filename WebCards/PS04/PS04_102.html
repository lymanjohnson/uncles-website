Misc. Notes
Ann Barlow was born probably around 1625-30 and probably in England.  She married Samuel Drake on September 13, 1650, at Fairfield [48].  This marriage was also recorded as September 30, 1650, by the Rev. Silas Constant of the Yorktown, New York, Presbyterian Church.  They moved to Eastchester, New York, in 1663 and Samuel Drake died there in 1686.  He was a son of John and Elizabeth (Rogers) Drake who came from England in 1630 [49].  Ann died after December 12, 1691 [50], probably at Eastchester.  Their children were: John Drake, born in 1654; Joseph Drake, born in 1663 and died on May 12, 1732; Rebecca Drake; Ruth Drake; Samuel Drake, died in 1690; Hannah Drake and Mary Drake.437
