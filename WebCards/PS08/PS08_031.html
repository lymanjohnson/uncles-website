Misc. Notes
Jasper Riddlesdale was born in Boxford, Suffolk, England around 1490. He married Elizabeth (last name UK). he was a husbandman (farmer) who also dealt in selling clay and gravel. He was involved in church affairs, being a church warden and arranging "church ale's" (social gatherings).

He and his wife had eleven children; Henry (abt 1518-1591), Thomas (abt 1522-1567), Peter, Grace KEBLE, Elizabeth RYVET, Jane, Amy, Robert, Richard, John (abt 1536-1614) and William. Jasper died in 1552 followed by his wife, Elizabeth, a year later.
Will
... to each of my daughters, namely Grace, Elizabeth, Jane and Amy £4 apiece to be paid by the hands of Elizabeth my wife and Henry my son, save that 4s. 10d. which shall be paid of the debts that her husband Richard Keble owes me... to Thomas my son my house & land with appurtenances called Dove's lying in a street of Boxford called Hagmer rye, the same Thomas my son paying to Richard & John his brothers £20, £10 apiece of good and lawful money, 33s. 4d. apiece every year, that is £3.6s.8d. to both till the sum of £20 be fully paid ... the first payment within the first year after he enter and take possession of the same house & land ... money to be held by executors until they reach age 21 ... houses & land in Polstead called Osmonds to Elizabeth my wife for life, then to son William ... Elizabeth my wife and Henry my son to have my farm in Boxford, Henry paying to Robert my son his brother £10 within one year after he enter the place .. Elizabeth my wife & Henry my son to have all my corn, hay, horses, sheep, cattle ... Thomas Rydysdale and Henry Redysdale my sons and Thomas Ryvett, parson of Boxford, to be supervisors & each to have 20s. ... Alice Bygg my kinswoman to have 3 yards of my red cloth ... to every godchild 4d. ... to the poor men's box of Boxford 3s. 4d. ... I forgive Peter my son the 5 marks that he owes me ... I forgive my sister Agnes 13s. that she owes me ... to my 2 sisters Christine Egle and Margery Skott, to each of them 6s. 4d. ... to Thomas Ryddsdale the son of Peter Rydysdale 2 sheep ... to Peter my son £3. 6s. 8d. to be paid him by Thomas Rydysdale and Henry Hydysdale my executors.
