Misc. Notes
On Mar 13 1635/6 Sarah married George Sutton, in Scituate, MA. Born ca 1613 in England. George was baptized in Tenterdon, Kent on poss. 12 Apr 1613. George died on 12 Apr 1669 in Albemarle Co., Perquimans District, NC.

"George Sutton had lands laid out in 1638, on the 1st. Herring brook [in Scituate]. His house was near John Daman's at the south-west end of Green field lane, where it unites with the Drift way."21
