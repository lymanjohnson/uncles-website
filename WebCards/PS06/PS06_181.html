Name Capt. Micael PIERCE197, v3p430
Death 26 Mar 1676, Rehoboth, Bristol, MA
Misc. Notes
Hingham, 1646, had there baptized that year Persis; other children... all named in his will, beside probably Abigail, born 1662 when his wife died. Soon after, he removed to Scituate, took second wife Ann; was a captain of great bravery, in command of 50 English and 20 friendly Indians from Cape Cod, in Philip's war, and was with most of them killed 26 Mar 1676, at Pawtucket fight in Rehoboth. Deane, 122,325
