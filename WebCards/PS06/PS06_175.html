Misc. Notes
Scituate, first town clerk 1636, Married Lydia, daughter of elder Nathaniel Tilden, had Joseph, born 1648; John 1651; Nary 1655; abd Richard 1659. Deane supposes him to have been son of Richard of Charlestown, in which he must be mistaken; but may be nearer to probalbe in thinking him that Richard who died at Boston, 29 Mar 1662.197, v2p233-4

"Dea. Richard Garrett was in Scituate before 1636. He was the first Town Clerk, 1636, also clerk of the Conihassett partners ten years, though not a partner himself, being a skillful penman. His land was nearly in front of the north or first Society's Meeting-house, as it now [1831] stands, which was probably his house lot. Also east of Eagle's nest swamp, where his sons John and Joseph settled."577
