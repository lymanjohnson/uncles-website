Misc. Notes
came in the "Ann" 1623, prob. a youth, of wh. all that is kn. seems to be, that in 1652 he was one of the purch. of Darmouth, and d. soon aft. for inv. of his prop. present. by Bathsheba, prob. his wid. 9 Oct 1656, was only of the amo. £18 11s. 3d. Perhaps he had ch. but the gr.s. Ephraim, by Farmer believ. to be b. Nov 1687, and his s. Michael, wonders of old age in 1804 and 1826, are by Barry render. wholly improb.197, v3p474

Bathsheba married 2nd, 29 Aug, 1667, John Dogged [POM]. Seems to be that in 1652, was one of the purchasers of Dartmouth[DG].209
