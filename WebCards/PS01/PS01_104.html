Misc. Notes
Went to NY soon after his marriage, where he reared most of his children. Removed to Michigan in 1846, settling in Highland Township.

Originally a Democrat but when his four sons (Oscar, Willis, Lyman Jr., and Leonard) became old enough to vote, he changed to a Republican with them.499

According to the publication "Portrait and Biographical Album of Oakland Co. MI" he was born Feb. 6, 1800.
