197ELISHA, Salem, m. Rachel Cook. had John, b. 1 Feb. 1668; Edward, 2 Feb. 1670; Elisha, 2 Mar. 1673, d. young; and James, 27 Dec. 1675; rem. to Enfield 1682, then pt. of Springfield, had Isaac, the first male ch. in E. b. 21 Mar 1683; and Rachel, 17 Apr. 1688; and he may have had others bef. He d. 3 Apr. 1735, and tradit. with its aptness to exagger. made him 97; but, in 1693, he calls hims. a "48 yrs." Perhaps he was s. of the preced. or of the foll. 197
